Reliable: Revolutionizing Auto Workshop Experience

An innovative platform to find mechanics, book appointments, and share feedback effortlessly.

About RAWR

RAWR is a system designed to make finding and booking appointments with mechanics easier. It provides users with the ability to search for nearby mechanic shops, schedule services, and leave ratings and feedback. With four dedicated portals—Manager Portal, Customer Portal, Admin Portal, and Shop Owner Profile—Reliable simplifies workshop management and enhances user experience.

How We Created RAWR?

Step 1: Planning

We started by identifying the challenges faced by users and workshop owners. Research and surveys helped shape the vision for a seamless mechanic locator and booking system.

Step 2: Designing

Using Figma, We created user-friendly designs for all four portals, ensuring the interfaces catered to both technical and non-technical users.

Step 3: Development

We built Reliable using modern tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React for the front end, and Node.js for the backend, ensuring robust and scalable performance.

Step 4: Testing & Deployment

We rigorously tested the system to ensure reliability across all portals. Reliable was successfully deployed in 2024, providing an end-to-end solution for mechanics and customers.

Features of RAWR

  • Mechanic Locator: Find nearby mechanic shops with ease.
  • Appointment Booking: Schedule services directly through the platform.
  • Ratings and Feedback: Share your experience to help others choose the best services.
  • Dedicated Portals: Separate interfaces for managers, customers, admins, and shop owners.
