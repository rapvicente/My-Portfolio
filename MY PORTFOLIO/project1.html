About MotoMaster

I created MotoMaster as a mobile app tailored for motor enthusiasts, offering a combination of utility and entertainment. This app features trivia quizzes, a marketplace for motor parts, service recommendations, and a locator for nearby mechanics. MotoMaster represents my dedication to bridging the gap between passion and practicality.

How I Created MotoMaster?

Step 1: Planning

I began by identifying the challenges faced by motor enthusiasts. Conducting surveys and gathering user insights were critical steps in designing an app that directly addresses their needs.

Step 2: Designing

I used Figma to sketch and prototype the app's interface, ensuring a modern, user-friendly design. A clean and intuitive layout was the goal, keeping usersâ€™ needs at the center of every design decision.

Step 3: Development

Using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React Native, I built MotoMaster from scratch. I implemented a robust backend using Node.js to ensure smooth performance for every feature.

Step 4: Testing & Deployment

I performed rigorous testing to iron out bugs and optimize the app. Finally, I successfully deployed MotoMaster in 2024, marking the culmination of countless hours of hard work.

Features of MotoMaster

  • Trivia Quizzes: Expand your motor knowledge with engaging quizzes.
  • Marketplace: Buy and sell motor products with ease.
  • Service Recommendations: Discover the best parts and services tailored to your needs.
  • Mechanic Locator: Find trusted mechanics near you in moments.
